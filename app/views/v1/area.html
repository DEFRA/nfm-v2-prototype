What Causes Flooding in you area

There are lots of different types of flooding. Water can come from lots of different sources spread over a wide area.

The main causes of flooding in your area are:

  • River flooding from the rivers that feed the River Mersey. Eg Sankey Brook, River Glaze, and the River Birket.
  • Tidal flooding, especially along the Mersey Estuary and around the Wirral Peninsula
  • Surface Water flooding, especially in urban areas Eg. Liverpool, Warrington, Ashton in Makerfield and some areas of Wirral
  • Sewer flooding can happen in all areas, but especially Hindley and Prescot
