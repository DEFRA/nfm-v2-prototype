The causes of flooding in your area

There are lots of different types of flooding. Water can come from lots of different sources spread over a wide area. This area is called a catchment.

The main causes of flooding in your area are:

  • River flooding from the rivers that feed the River Mersey. Eg Sankey Brook, River Glaze, and the River Birket.
  • Tidal flooding, especially along the Mersey Estuary and around the Wirral Peninsula
  • Surface Water flooding, especially in urban areas Eg. Liverpool, Warrington, Ashton in Makerfield and some areas of Wirral
  • Sewer flooding can happen in all areas, but especially Hindley and Prescot

How to help reduce flood risk

There are many ways to reduce flood risk to an area. The first things people think of are often large scale, hard engineered flood walls and other river mangement schemes. These can be very effective in the right areas, but they are hugely expensive, disruptive and environmentally damaging.

Large scale, engineered soultions aren't suitable for every area. Even if they are in place, they may not be effective used on their own, especially as we see more rain every year. There are other, smaller scale measure that can help.

Natural Flood Management

Natural Flood Management (NFM), sometimes known as Working with Natural Processes or Nature Based Soultions, is a number of smaller scale techniques that enhance the effectiveness of traditional flood defence mechanisms. Their aim is to catch and slow water as it flows so that it doesn't all meet at the same time during floods.
