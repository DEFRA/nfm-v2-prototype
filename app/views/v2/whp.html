Multiple benefits

By working with natural processes you can also:

  • increase biodiversity
  • improve water quality
  • improve soil health
  • capture carbon

Make small changes Use small scale solutions

You can contribute to capturing water and slowing its by:

  • installing water butts
  • reduce hard paving around your property
  • install green roofs
  • building ponds and rain gardens
  • use leftover soil to build mounds

Find out more about how to use these sustainable drainage systems.

Work together

By working together with your local communities and non-governmental organisations you can get involved in a wide range of solutions.

Even if you don’t experience flooding, using natural flood management can help those who do as actions in one location can lead to significant results in another location.
