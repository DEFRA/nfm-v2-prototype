Natural Flood Management

Prototypes for NFM 2.0

11 February, 2021

Version 1

The first iterations of ideas after discovery. User research gave us a lot of directions to explore, but also some common themes. The two iterations explore solutions that stick to the mission statement of 'raising awareness of NFM' and not getting distracted by trying to solve all the problems.

Option A

Option A explores the use of a location service to provide local context to flooding as a whole, before providing information about NFM as a solution.

Option B

Option B is a pure content based solution to raise awareness of NFM - the designs of which are based on the style of the 'Getting help with child arrangements' guide.
