29 February, 2021

Version 1.1

Version 1.1 Details

After the first stage of user research around Version 1 this is a tweaked version based on some of things we noticed.

Option A

Option A still explores the use of a location service to provide local context, but the 'story' is made a bit tighter. Also the content pages are pared back so they are more different top Option B. This makes sure that there is a clear distinction between the layouts for the user.

Option B

Option B remains largely the same, but we have rearranged the order of the sections to see if making the NFM section more prevalent has any effect. There are also some extra navgation cues at the bottom of sections to aid the journey through the story steps.

11 February, 2021

Version 1

Version 1 Details

The first iterations of ideas after discovery. User research gave us a lot of directions to explore, but also some common themes. The two iterations explore solutions that stick to the mission statement of 'raising awareness of NFM' and not getting distracted by trying to solve all the problems.

Option A

Option A explores the use of a location service to provide local context to flooding as a whole, before providing information about NFM as a solution.

Option B

Option B is a pure content based solution to raise awareness of NFM - the designs of which are based on the style of the 'Getting help with child arrangements' guide.
